Ingredients
  • $1$ cup flour ($120 \text{g}$)
  • $1/2$ cup sugar ($100 \text{g}$)
  • $2$ eggs
  • $1$ teaspoon vanilla extract
  • $1/4$ teaspoon salt ($\text{NaCl}$)

Check your pantry for these essentials before starting!

Steps
  1. Preheat oven to $350^\circ\text{F}$ ($175^\circ\text{C}$).
  2. In a large bowl, whisk together the dry ingredients ($\text{flour}$, $\text{sugar}$, $\text{salt}$).
  3. In a separate bowl, combine the wet ingredients.
  4. Slowly mix wet and dry ingredients until just combined.
  5. Pour into a greased $9 \text{x} 5$ inch pan.
Cooking Instructions

Bake for $45$ minutes. Check for doneness by inserting a toothpick; it should come out clean. Let cool for $15$ minutes before serving.
